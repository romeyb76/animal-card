Panther

Panthers are generally quiet and cautious animals.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Onca
  • Average Length: 43in - 75in
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: Forest, swampland and grasslands

The panther is an elusive and powerful animal and is known to be one of the strongest climbers of all felines. Their dark fur camouflages them both into the surrounding forest and makes them almost invisible in the darkness of night. A-Z Animals
